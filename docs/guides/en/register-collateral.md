# How to Register Your Livestock as Collateral

This guide walks you through registering your animals on StellarKraal so you can use them to get a loan. You do not need any technical knowledge — just follow the steps below.

---

## What you will need

- A smartphone or computer with internet access
- The **Freighter** browser extension (free to install)
- Details about your animals: type, number, weight, location, and estimated value

---

## Step 1 — Install the Freighter wallet

Freighter is a free digital wallet that keeps your account safe. You only need to install it once.

1. Open your web browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended).
2. Go to [freighter.app](https://www.freighter.app) and click **Add to Chrome** (or Firefox).
3. Click **Add extension** when your browser asks for permission.
4. Once installed, click the Freighter icon in your browser toolbar.
5. Choose **Create a new wallet**.
6. Write down the 12-word recovery phrase on paper and keep it somewhere safe. **Never share this phrase with anyone.**
7. Set a password for your wallet and click **Confirm**.

> **Tip:** If you already have a Freighter wallet, skip to Step 2.

---

## Step 2 — Connect your wallet to StellarKraal

1. Open StellarKraal in your browser.
2. On the home page, click the **Connect Wallet** button.
3. A Freighter pop-up will appear asking for permission. Click **Connect**.
4. Your wallet address (a long string of letters and numbers) will appear at the top of the page. This confirms you are connected.

> **Note:** Make sure Freighter is set to **Testnet** while you are trying things out. You can change the network inside the Freighter extension under Settings → Network.

---

## Step 3 — Open the collateral registration form

1. From the top menu, click **Collateral**.
2. Click the **Register New Collateral** button.
3. The registration form will open.

---

## Step 4 — Fill in the registration form

Fill in each field as described below. Fields marked with a red star (\*) are required.

| Field | What to enter |
|---|---|
| **Animal Type** | Choose from the list: Cattle, Goat, or Sheep |
| **Quantity** | The number of animals you are registering (e.g. 5) |
| **Estimated Weight (kg)** | The average weight of one animal in kilograms (e.g. 250) |
| **Health Status** | Choose the health of your animals: Excellent, Good, Fair, or Poor |
| **Location** | The name of your farm or region (e.g. Narok County) |
| **Appraised Value** | The total estimated value of your animals in stroops (see note below) |

> **What is a stroop?** A stroop is the smallest unit of XLM (Stellar Lumens), the currency used on the Stellar network. 1 XLM = 10,000,000 stroops. If your animals are worth 10 XLM, enter 100,000,000.

> **Auto-save:** The form saves your progress automatically every 5 seconds. If you close the page by accident, you will be asked if you want to restore your work when you return.

---

## Step 5 — Submit and sign the transaction

1. Once all fields are filled in, click **Register Collateral**.
2. A confirmation box will appear showing a summary of what you are registering. Check the details carefully.
3. Click **Register** to confirm.
4. Freighter will open and ask you to sign the transaction. Click **Approve**.
5. Wait a few seconds while the registration is processed.
6. You will see a green message: **Collateral registered successfully!** along with a registration ID. Write this ID down — you may need it later.

---

## Step 6 — Understanding the appraisal

After registration, your collateral goes through an **appraisal** — an automatic check that estimates how much your animals are worth on the Stellar network.

- The appraisal value determines how large a loan you can request.
- A higher health status and heavier animals generally result in a higher appraisal.
- The appraisal is updated periodically. You can see the current value on your **Collateral** page.
- You can borrow up to a percentage of the appraised value (the exact percentage is shown on the loan request page).

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| "Connect Wallet" button does nothing | Make sure the Freighter extension is installed and you are logged in |
| Freighter pop-up does not appear | Check that your browser allows pop-ups from this site |
| Form shows red error messages | Read the message next to the field and correct the value |
| Transaction fails | Check your internet connection and try again. If the problem continues, open a [support issue](https://github.com/teslims2/StellarKraal-/issues) |
| You forgot your wallet password | Use your 12-word recovery phrase to restore your wallet in Freighter |
